Frequently Asked Questions

How does Devi calculate panchang data?

Devi uses the Swiss Ephemeris astronomical library with Lahiri ayanamsa to compute all panchang elements (tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana) in real-time on your device. No internet connection is required.

Why does my city show different times than another panchang app?

Panchang times are location-specific. Sunrise, sunset, and all derived time windows depend on your city's latitude, longitude, and timezone. Make sure you've selected the correct city in Settings.

How do I change my city?

Open Settings (gear icon) and tap "City" to choose from popular cities or search for any city worldwide.

Does Devi work offline?

Yes. All astronomical calculations are performed on-device. You only need internet for the initial city search (if searching for a non-listed city).

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings and toggle the notification types you want (sunrise, sunset, Rahu Kalam, etc.). If notifications are disabled at the system level, tap the "Notifications Disabled" banner to open iOS Settings.
