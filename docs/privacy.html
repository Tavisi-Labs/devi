Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Devi is a Hindu Panchang (almanac) app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data Devi collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Devi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • Location: If you grant permission, your device's location is used locally to determine your nearest city for accurate sunrise/sunset and panchang calculations. Your coordinates are never sent to any server.
  • City Selection: Your selected city is stored on-device using UserDefaults. It is not transmitted externally.
  • Notification Preferences: Your notification settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Devi does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Devi does not have a backend server, user accounts, or cloud storage.

Children's Privacy

Devi does not collect any personal information and is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes, we will update this page with the new effective date.
